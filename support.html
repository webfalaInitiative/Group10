Support for Survivors

At SafeWomen Initiatives, we are committed to providing support and resources for survivors of Sexual and Gender-Based Violence (SGBV). If you or someone you know has experienced SGBV, it's essential to know that you are not alone, and help is available. Below are various forms of support and resources you can access

1

Counseling Services
