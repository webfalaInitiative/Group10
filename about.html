Our Mission

At SafeWomen Initiatives, our mission is to break the cycle of silence and stigma surrounding SGBV by providing accessible resources, fostering community dialogue, and advocating for systemic change.

Our vision

Create a world where gender-based violence is not tolerated and survivors are empowered and supported

Our Team

Behind SafeWomen Initiatives is a dedicated team of individuals who bring a diverse range of experiences, expertise, and perspectives to our work.

As the visionary behind SafeHer she is driven by a personal commitment to social justice and a desire to create positive change in the world. With a background in advocacy and community organizing, she brings leadership, compassion, and unwavering determination to our mission.

Responsible for curating and creating impactful content for our website and social media platform she is dedicated to amplifying the voices of survivors and raising awareness about the complexities of SGBV, she brings creativity, empathy, and a keen eye for detail to our team.

Leading our advocacy efforts and strategic partnerships, she is passionate about driving systemic change to end SGBV and promote gender equality. With experience in policy analysis and grassroots organizing,she is a tireless advocate for survivors' rights and social justice.
